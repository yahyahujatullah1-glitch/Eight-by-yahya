Users are strictly prohibited from:

Privacy Policy

Your data security is our priority.

1. Data Collection

To ensure server security and prevent cheating, we collect specific hardware information (HWID), IP addresses, and Discord identifiers upon connection. This data is used solely for authentication and moderation.

2. Voice Data

Eight RP utilizes proximity voice chat. While we do not actively record all voice conversations, voice data is processed in real-time to facilitate gameplay. Reports involving voice harassment may be reviewed by administration.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Limited data may be shared with payment processors (e.g., Stripe/PayPal) solely for the purpose of completing optional donations.
