Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Eight RP (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you play the Eight RP mobile roleplay game (“Game”). By installing or using Eight RP, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate and improve the Game.

1.1 Account Information

  • Username / Player ID
  • Email (if provided)
  • Device ID

1.2 Gameplay Information

  • In-game actions and statistics
  • Level, progress, achievements
  • Logs related to rule violations, bans, or reports

1.3 Technical Information

  • Device type (Android/iOS)
  • IP address
  • App version
  • Crash reports and performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Operate and maintain the Game
  • Improve gameplay performance and stability
  • Detect cheating, exploits, or rule violations
  • Provide support and help resolve issues
  • Manage player safety and enforce rules
  • Process optional in-app purchases

We do not sell your data.

3. Data Sharing

We may share limited data only with:

  • Payment processors (for in-app purchases)
  • Analytics or crash-report services
  • Law enforcement if legally required

We never sell or trade your personal information.

4. Children’s Privacy

Eight RP is not intended for children under 13 (or minimum legal age in your region). We do not knowingly collect data from children below this age.

5. Data Protection

We take security seriously and use:

  • Encrypted connections
  • Secure data storage
  • Restricted staff access

However, no system is 100% secure, and you use the Game at your own risk.

6. Player Rights

Depending on your country, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request corrections
  • Withdraw consent (by uninstalling the Game)

To request these, contact us using the information below.

7. In-App Purchases

If you make purchases:

  • Payment information is processed by the app store (Google Play / Apple App Store).
  • We do not store your credit card or banking details.

8. Cookies & Tracking

Eight RP does not use cookies, but may use:

  • Local device storage
  • Analytics tracking for performance and crash reporting

No personal browsing data is collected.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. When you continue using the Game after changes, you accept the updated Policy.

10. Contact Information

If you have questions, concerns, or requests:

Email: support@eightrp.com
Discord: Join Server

By using Eight RP, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
